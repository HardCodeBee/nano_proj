# E3 Synthetic Distillation Plan

## Goal

Move beyond incremental sampler-only gains by introducing a higher-quality synthetic
ROC-style short-story corpus generated by a stronger model, then distill that corpus into
the small checkpoint before a final ROCStories polish.

This is the current aggressive Task 2 branch.

## Why this branch is worth trying

- E1 and E2 improved daily `ROC val` `ppl`, but story-quality gains were modest
- Task 3 will reward both `PPL` and judged story quality
- a cleaner ROC-style synthetic corpus has a better chance of changing story-level behavior than another tiny sampler tweak

## Pipeline

### Stage A: generate synthetic ROC-style stories

Script:
[generate_rocstories_synthetic.py](C:/Users/12442/Desktop/GitHub/nano_proj/scripts/generate_rocstories_synthetic.py)

Prompt file:
[task2_rocstyle_rewrite_prompt.txt](C:/Users/12442/Desktop/GitHub/nano_proj/prompts/task2_rocstyle_rewrite_prompt.txt)

Recommended first run:

```bash
export OPENAI_API_KEY="your_new_key"
./.venv/bin/python scripts/generate_rocstories_synthetic.py \
  --api-key "$OPENAI_API_KEY" \
  --base-url "https://api.openai.com/v1" \
  --model "gpt-4o-mini" \
  --target-count 3000 \
  --source-limit 12000 \
  --resume
```

## Success criteria

Compared with E2, look for:

- lower `ROC val` `avg_loss` / `ppl`
- at least some `3` scores from the local automatic judge
- fewer bland, repetitive, or semantically drifting fixed-prompt outputs
